Why TikTok Is the New Radio for Music Discovery

Tim Elliot March 15, 2024

The Shift in Music Discovery

A decade ago, radio and Spotify playlists broke new artists. Now, that power lives inside a 15-second video.

TikTok has turned music discovery into a cultural event. Songs don't just get released anymore, they get revealed through trends, sounds, and shared emotions. Every scroll is a stage, every creator a promoter.

When your track fits the right moment, it explodes, not because of luck, but because of connection. That's why even major labels now design releases around TikTok-first moments.

From Underground to Viral

TikTok's algorithm rewards authenticity and emotional resonance. It's not about polished visuals or high budgets, it's about vibe, story, and timing. Artists like PinkPantheress, JVKE, and Steve Lacy built entire careers on this.

Your song can reach millions overnight, if it lands where culture moves.
